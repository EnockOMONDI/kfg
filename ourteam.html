DR. ELSIE OMBAKHO.

Co founder

Dr. Elsie Ombakho is a Medical Doctor and Public Health Practitioner. With over four years of clinical experience as a dedicated healthcare practitioner, Elsie has seamlessly transitioned into the realm of public health, driven by a passion for improving healthcare processes and enhancing the overall well-being of communities.

During Elsie's clinical practice, a profound interest in women's health and obstetrics/gynecology emerged, particularly in the maternity ward. Witnessing the strength and resilience of women, who often bear the weight of familial responsibilities, inspired Elsie to delve deeper into empowering women.

Currently involved in a transformative project in collaboration with The Kenyan Feminine Goddess, Elsie is working to empower girls and women in rural Kenya to pursue entrepreneurship. By harnessing their skills, these women are empowered to generate income and create positive change within their communities. Elsie firmly believes in the inseparable link between women's health and empowerment. Healthy women, armed with knowledge about their well-being, become formidable forces capable of driving societal development. Join Elsie and The Kenyan feminine Goddess on the panel as they share insights and experiences, advocating for a holistic approach to women's health and empowerment.

Dr. Loise Kabiru

Chief Operating Officer

Dr. Loise Kabiru is a distinguished medical doctor and an enthusiastic advocate for global public health. She stands as a beacon of empowerment within the Goddess Care Initiative. With her unwavering commitment and expertise, Dr. Loise plays a pivotal role in our mission to uplift and support women and children in rural Kenya.

As a trusted advisor and ally, Dr. Kabiru's guidance and advocacy resonate deeply with our mission to foster positive change and create opportunities for women's advancement. Her involvement underscores our collective commitment to building a more equitable and inclusive society, where every woman and girl has the opportunity to fulfill her potential.

Kelly Shitambasi

Founder and Creator

Kelly Shitambasi is a visionary leader dedicated to empowering women in Kenya and beyond. As a cancer survivor, Kelly's own journey inspired her to create The Kenyan Feminine Goddess, a platform aimed at inspiring women to embrace their strength and resilience. Through her thought-provoking advice column, Kelly fosters a community of support and empowerment, reminding women of their worth and potential.

Kelly is a tireless advocate for gender equality and women's rights. She spearheads the Goddess Care Initiative, aimed at uplifting women and girls in rural Kenya by providing them with resources and opportunities to thrive. Through this initiative, Kelly encourages women to leverage their skills and talents, creating pathways to economic independence and self-empowerment.

Kelly's unwavering commitment to advancing the status of women in society makes her a true champion of change. She is working tirelessly to achieve UN Sustainable Development Goal 3: Good Health and Well-being, ensuring that women everywhere have access to quality healthcare and support.

Kenyan Feminine Goddess is a dedicated platform designed to inspire and empower women during times of uncertainty, despair, and turmoil. Through shared experiences, profound insights, and timeless wisdom, we aim to provide valuable support and inspiration to women.
