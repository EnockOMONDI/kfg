DR. ELSIE OMBAKHO.

Co founder

Dr. Elsie Ombakho is a Medical Doctor and Public Health Practitioner. With over four years of clinical experience as a dedicated healthcare practitioner, Elsie has seamlessly transitioned into the realm of public health, driven by a passion for improving healthcare processes and enhancing the overall well-being of communities.

During Elsie's clinical practice, a profound interest in women's health and obstetrics/gynecology emerged, particularly in the maternity ward. Witnessing the strength and resilience of women, who often bear the weight of familial responsibilities, inspired Elsie to delve deeper into empowering women.

Currently involved in a transformative project in collaboration with The Kenyan Feminine Goddess, Elsie is working to empower girls and women in rural Kenya to pursue entrepreneurship. By harnessing their skills, these women are empowered to generate income and create positive change within their communities. Elsie firmly believes in the inseparable link between women's health and empowerment. Healthy women, armed with knowledge about their well-being, become formidable forces capable of driving societal development. Join Elsie and The Kenyan feminine Goddess on the panel as they share insights and experiences, advocating for a holistic approach to women's health and empowerment.
